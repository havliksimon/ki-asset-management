Investment Approach

Our Strategy Pillars

A disciplined approach combining emerging markets exposure with small-mid cap opportunities and compelling equity stories.

No Leverage

We avoid leverage to prevent increased volatility and drawdowns. Asymmetric risk with unlimited loss potential has no place in our strategy.

No Shorts

Short selling introduces margin call risks and borrow costs. We prefer allocating time to finding winners rather than speculating on losers.

No Hedging

We don't hedge currencies as they provide natural diversification. FX risk is monitored during high volatility periods.

Non-Replicable Strategy

Our portfolio cannot be replicated by standard ETFs. We actively select and manage 15-25 positions for true diversification, not "di-worse-ification".
