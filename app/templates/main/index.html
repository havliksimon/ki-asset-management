Investment Approach

Our Strategy Pillars

A disciplined approach combining emerging markets exposure with small-mid cap opportunities and compelling equity stories.

No Leverage

We avoid leverage to prevent increased volatility and drawdowns. Asymmetric risk with unlimited loss potential has no place in our strategy.

No Shorts

Short selling introduces margin call risks and borrow costs. We prefer allocating time to finding winners rather than speculating on losers.

No Hedging

We don't hedge currencies as they provide natural diversification. FX risk is monitored during high volatility periods.

Non-Replicable Strategy

Our portfolio cannot be replicated by standard ETFs. We actively select and manage 15-25 positions for true diversification, not "di-worse-ification".

Risk Framework

Risk Management

Robust risk controls ensuring capital preservation and sustainable returns through disciplined position sizing and continuous monitoring.

Position Sizing

Maximum 10% of portfolio per position at cost basis. Growth tolerance up to 20% before trimming. Gradual entry with max 5% initial allocation.

Diversification

Maximum 20% exposure per sector and 30% per region. Portfolio of 15-25 positions for optimal diversification without over-concentration.

Stop-Loss Policy

Soft stop-loss at 20% decline triggers thesis review by independent analyst. Regular revalidation of all portfolio positions on monthly basis.

Liquidity Control

Position size limited to 5x average daily trading volume. Preference for dual-listed securities to minimize currency and liquidity risk.

Cash Management

Excess cash held in interest-bearing accounts (CZK, EUR, USD). Higher cash allocation during elevated valuations, deployment during corrections.

Consensus Requirement

Absolute consensus required for all investment decisions. Any dissenting vote blocks position entry until concerns are addressed.

Investment Universe

Where We Invest

Focused on market segments where our student-driven approach provides genuine competitive advantage.

Small/Mid Caps

Market capitalization under $25B. Under-covered by analysts, too small for hedge funds. Structural inefficiencies create mispricing opportunities for diligent researchers.

Emerging Markets

CEE, LATAM, and Asia exposure. Our regional expertise, language skills, and Erasmus network provide local insights overlooked by Western investors.

Equity Stories

Special situations, thematic investments, and contrarian opportunities. Market skepticism or neglect creating asymmetric risk/reward profiles.

Exclusion Criteria
Harmful Industries

Tobacco, weapons, gambling, and companies causing significant environmental or social harm

"Cursed" Sectors

Chronically low ROIC industries like airlines with structural profitability challenges

Dishonest Management

Any history of accounting fraud, integrity failures, or governance red flags
